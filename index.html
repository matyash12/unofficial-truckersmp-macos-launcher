Version 1.0.2
Use at your own resposibility!

Basic how-to tutorial. READ BEFORE USE!

When you press on button, please wait for at least 10 seconds. It takes time.


Step 1.
When you first start this app click on "Click to install Wine, Steam and other packages".
It will install wine, Steam for Windows and some other packages which this app needs.

Step 2.
Do this step when "Click to open Steam" and "Click to open TrucksMP laucnher" buttons become blue.
Click on "Click to open Steam" to open Steam. Log in and download Euro Truck Simulator 2.
When download is finished click on "Click to kill all wine processes". This will close Steam.

Step 3.
Click on "Click to open TruckersMP launcher".
This will open Unofficial TrucksMP launcher. Log in and play!.

Known issues.

When you press "esc", while driving the game often crashes. Use "r" or "m" to go to radio or map and then go home "home icon".
